Features

Signup

This feature allows new users to create an account on Selam. Users will be asked to provide their name, email address, and a password. Once the user has created an account, they will be able to log in to Selam and start using the platform.

Login

This feature allows users to log in to their Selam account. Users will be asked to provide their email address and password. Once the user has logged in, they will be able to access their profile, post content, and interact with other users.

Feeds

This feature allows users to view posts from other users on Selam. Users will be able to scroll through their feed and see posts from users they follow. Users will also be able to like, comment, and share posts with their followers.
